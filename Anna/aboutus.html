International.ltd is a company which specializes in dealing with professional services related to immigration. International. ltd is a privately-owned Canadian Immigration Consultancy Agency, based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada and other provinces. The agency assists individuals and families around the world with their visa application and immigration process.

Our goal:

to help you move to Canada without violating Canadian law.

Our task:

to provide expert advice in the selection of the immigration program. At the conclusion of the contract-legal support of all immigration processes.

Our concept:

      - free evaluation of chances on the company's website;
      - we do not hide or withhold information about the real prospects of a particular direction;
      - we do not charge the client in advance, only after submitting documents to the immigration office;
      - in case of refusal due to the Agency's fault, the subsequent submission of documents is at our expense.

Accreditation:
